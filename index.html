BloodMoney 2, also known as the Human Expenditure Program, is a psychological horror simulation game that is a sequel to the dark indie title BLOODMONEY!.

How to Play BloodMoney 2

Playing BloodMoney 2 is a tense and thought-provoking experience. Your goal is to care for Harvey Harvington, the game's central character, who now exists as a lost computer program.

You are getting PAID to perform basic interactions like feed and pet, but every choice has a lasting consequences. The game will fill you with guilt as you grapple with the past, forcing you to think carefully before deciding on your actions. The game also features five different minigames that offer unique challenges and break up the tension.
